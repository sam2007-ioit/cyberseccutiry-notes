                      according to organization's demand 


controls : measures taken to protect the organization data, it is of main 3 types 
           A. Encryption : to convert the normal text in to cipher text so that not everyone can access the imfromation 
           B. Authentication : to check wether the person accessing a information is real or an threat actor
           C. Authorization : to avoid ordinary people from accessing the confidential data by only leting authorized people access them 
           Examples of physical controls:
              cctv, watchman, doors, locks
           Examples of technical controls:
              antivirus software, MFA
